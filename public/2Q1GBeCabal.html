Tips for Choosing the Right Club

Joining a club is one of the best ways to explore your interests, and meet new friends. At PSHS MC, there are so much clubs to choose from, ranging from STEM to arts, sports, performing, and recreation.

Here are some tips to help you choose the right club:

  • Follow your passion. Choose a club that aligns with your interests or goals.
  • Try something new. Don’t be afraid! Explore unfamiliar clubs as you might discover a hidden talent.
  • Check the schedule. Make sure the club’s activities don’t clash with your academic responsibilities.
  • Talk to members. Ask upper batches or club officers about their experiences before joining.
  • Start small. You don’t need to join multiple clubs at once. Begin with one and see how it goes.
